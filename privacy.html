Privacy Policy

Agreeable Ventures LLC · Last updated July 2026

Agreeable Ventures LLC ("Agreeable Ventures," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website at agreeableventures.com and our software products, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep this deliberately simple because our approach to data is simple: we collect as little as possible, and we do not sell it.

Information we collect

Our website does not require an account and does not track you across the web. If you use our contact form, we collect the name, email address, and message you choose to send us, so that we can respond. That is the only information the website itself collects from you directly.

Our software products collect the information a business and its customers enter in order to run the product, such as contact details, project information, and messages. When a business uses our products, that business controls its own data and its customers' data. We act as a processor of that data on the business's behalf.

Text messages

Where our products send text messages, we only send them to people who have opted in to receive them. Messages are transactional and sent on an as-needed basis in response to activity on your own project, such as appointment reminders, project status updates, and follow-ups. Message frequency varies and is typically low.

We do not share mobile phone numbers with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes, and we do not sell phone numbers. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes at any time. A recipient can reply STOP at any time to opt out, and we honor that immediately, or reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to respond to inquiries, to provide and operate our products, to communicate with users about their own accounts and projects, and to keep our products secure and working properly.

Aggregated and anonymized data

To improve our products and understand broad industry trends, we may use anonymized, aggregated data, for example general patterns in how businesses use features, typical project sizes, or conversion rates across many customers. This trend data never identifies you, your business, or your customers, and never includes your sensitive client or financial details. We keep trend data, not identifying data, for this purpose.

What we do not do

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share one customer's private data with another customer. We do not use your private business or client data to train products for anyone else.

Sharing with service providers

We rely on a small number of trusted service providers to operate, for example cloud hosting, database, and messaging providers. They process data only to provide their service to us and are bound to protect it. We do not authorize them to use your information for their own purposes.

Data security and retention

We use reputable infrastructure and industry-standard protections, including encryption in transit and at rest, to safeguard information. If a business closes its account with us, we retain its data for a limited window so the account can be restored if needed, and then remove it. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information diligently.

Your choices

You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. You can ask us what information we hold about you, and request correction or deletion, by emailing us at the address below. If you use one of our products through a business, you may also contact that business directly regarding its data.

Children

Our website and products are intended for businesses and adults. They are not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our products grow. When we do, we will change the date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated to account holders.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to james@agreeableventures.com. Agreeable Ventures LLC is based in Clarksville, Tennessee.
